    Hints and Hauls thread for Sour, Saison, and Funk Round 16! Post hints for your sender and target; along with general shenanigans.

    The rules:

    A box filled with lambic, wild ales, berliners, saisons, farmhouse ales, goses, etc., as well as anything else that is rustic or funky.
    • A 6 bottle shipper*
    • All beers must be an rAvg of 4.0 or higher or a 'want' by your target.**
    • Try to find fun and exciting bottles/growlers that your target otherwise wouldn't likely be able to try
    • Quantity is good, quality is better!
    * ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
    **If you can't hit wants, please try to find fun stuff that your target otherwise wouldn't likely be able to try.
