    Sorry for the delay, things came up at work and I got stuck much later than expected. Unflattering garage pics because these are all going straight in the fridge. box_social got me good w this box including some of my favorite funky breweries.

    https://i.imgur.com/NR9pu61_d.jpg?maxwidth=640&shape=thumb&fidelity=medium

    The de gawde bottles. Very excited since I haven’t had anything from them in awhile. A nice sante blend collab, gin BA nectarine banger, blended spontaneous ale (love their spontaneous blends so looking forward to this one!!) and raspberry spruce tip SP collab. No misses here, and I’ll be sure to chill the Long Tooth for a good amount of time before opening!

    https://i.imgur.com/nvlovpR_d.jpg?maxwidth=640&shape=thumb&fidelity=medium

    Next is the Upright goods. Not pictured is the BA wit growler which was crushed at work. I would not have guessed Pinot barrels but definitely got nice light vanilla oakiness to it. Overall it was fantastic and paired great with takis and secret aardvark. There’s a Dutch-inspired vermouth BA beer , anni saison which is a blend of open fermented cask aged saisons, and a saison aged in casks with gewürztraminer grapes (love anything with grapes so this is a hit!!). Upright is one of my favorite breweries in general and I have not had any of these before.

    https://i.imgur.com/jxPMtCm_d.jpg?maxwidth=640&shape=thumb&fidelity=medium

    And a nice mix of small format! My favorite geueze and a bottling date which I haven’t had before. The gigantic brew is a sour ale with orange and lemon aged in gin barrels (can’t get enough of the gin BA funk). And some block 15 treats! I haven’t had matryoshka in a few years but absolutely love this beer so it’s a very nice addition to this box. Can’t wait to try this dg collab though, was intrigued since I heard about it. Block 15 saison blended with de garde spontaneous beer sounds delightful. box_social i can’t thank you enough for an awesome selection of Oregon funk. Again these are some of my favorite breweries and I’m really looking forward to drinking my way through these over the next few weeks. None to be had tonight (other than the crowler) but I’ll be digging in this weekend. If you happen to need a sante proxy at any point just let me know!
